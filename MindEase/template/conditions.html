Mental Health Conditions

Learn about different mental health challenges and how to deal with them.

Depression

Symptoms:

  • Feelings of sadness, tearfulness, or hopelessness
  • Angry outbursts, irritability or frustration
  • Loss of interest or pleasure in most activities
  • Sleep disturbances, including insomnia
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Reduced appetite and weight loss
  • Feeling worthless or guilty
  • Trouble thinking or concentrating
  • Frequent thoughts of death or suicide

Anxiety

Symptoms:

  • Uneasy feeling, panic, or danger
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Unable to stay calm and still
  • Cold, sweaty or tingling hands or feet
  • Trouble breathing (both shortness of breath and breathing faster than normal)
  • Increased heart rate
  • Dry mouth
  • Dizziness or feeling weak
  • Can't stop thinking about a problem
  • Unable to concentrate
  • Strong fear of objects or places

PTSD

Post-traumatic stress disorder caused by experiencing trauma. Flashbacks and emotional distress are common.

OCD

Obsessive thoughts and repetitive behaviors. Requires cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT).

Bipolar Disorder

Mood disorder with emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression). Managed with therapy and medication.
