Did You Know?

  • One in four people in the world will be affected by mental or neurological disorders at some point in their lives.
  • Close to 1 billion people are living with a mental disorder globally.
  • Depression is a leading cause of disability worldwide.
  • Every 40 seconds, someone dies by suicide — that's over 800,000 people every year.

How You Can Help

  • Listen without judgment when someone opens up.
  • Encourage those struggling to seek professional help.
  • Support mental health organizations and spread awareness.
  • Educate yourself and others about mental health.
